Privacy Policy

GamHub Estate is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and your rights as a user in The Gambia.

Section 01

Who We Are

GamHub Estate is a property marketplace designed for buyers, sellers, renters and agents in The Gambia. We help people find, list and connect around property transactions across the country.

✉

Data Controller

gamhubestate@gmail.com
Section 02

What Data We Collect

Account Data: Email address used as your account identifier via Supabase Auth magic link.

Property Listing Data: Agent name, contact email, phone, property title, price, location, description, photos and amenities.

Seeker Data: Name, email, phone, property requirements and budget — only what you choose to post publicly.

Payment Data: We do not store card or mobile money details. All payments are processed by ModemPay.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

  • To publish and display your property listings or seeker profiles
  • To authenticate your account via Supabase magic link
  • To process payments via ModemPay for paid listing plans
  • To prevent abuse and track free listing allowances
We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.
Section 04

Your Rights

  • Right to access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: Email us to delete your account and all associated data.
  • Right to rectification: Update your listings and contact information at any time.

To exercise any rights, contact: gamhubestate@gmail.com

Listing Rules

  • Real listings only: You may only post genuine properties available for sale or rent.
